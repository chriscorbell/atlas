Phase 1

Foundation Setup

Establish the baseline for your homelab build by clarifying desired outcomes, hardware responsibilities, and rollout cadence. These mock steps outline the type of decisions you can capture here before replacing with your actual process notes.

Step 1.1: Scope the project

Draft lightweight briefs that describe success criteria, user personas, and acceptable risk boundaries. Placeholder language here can be swapped with your real discovery artifacts.

Step 1.2: Inventory hardware

Capture a table of candidate servers, storage, and networking gear along with power budgets and noise constraints. Substitute this text with details on your actual chassis, drives, and accessories.

Step 1.3: Map build timeline

Lay out a sprint-level plan that sequences procurement, assembly, burn-in, and services onboarding. Replace this filler narrative with your scheduling plan and dependencies.
